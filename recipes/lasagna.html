Lasagna

Description

This is the best lasagna recipe ever! This is the lasagna my grandmother used to make and it was passed down through the family for generations. I think you will like this very much. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Spahgetti sauce
  • Italian Sausage
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Fresh Basil
  1. Boil the lasagna noodles until they are just about al dente.
  2. Layer the noodles in 9 x 13 baking pan alternating layers of sauce and sausage
  3. Add a layer of Mozzerella cheese to the top layer
  4. Bake at 350F until bubbling, about 45 minutes
  5. Sprinkle the fresh basil on top just before serving
